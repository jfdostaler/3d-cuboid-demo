Instructions

Click the radio buttons at the top of the page to switch between the Surgeon and Pathologist measurements.

The shape will change size dynamically as coordinates are entered. Either press 'Enter', or select the next text box, for the change to take effect.

Camera controls:

  • Left mouse button + drag = rotate
  • Right mouse button + drag = pan
  • Middle button + drag (or scroll wheel) = zoom
